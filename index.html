Projects

  • IOT Based Agriculture DC Motor Control by Android Application

    • In modern agriculture, efficient water management is crucial for sustainable farming. Our project, "IoT-Based Agriculture DC Motor Control by Android Application," introduces a smart solution for farmers to remotely monitor and control their irrigation systems. This system integrates IoT technology with a DC motor, allowing users to operate the motor through an Android application. The app provides real-time data on motor runtime, water suction speed, and water level, ensuring optimal water usage. The system enhances efficiency by reducing manual intervention, preventing water wastage, and enabling remote access. With this smart irrigation solution, farmers can improve productivity, save resources, and achieve precision agriculture effortlessly.

  • Smart wireless water meter with web DB IoT

    • Water management is a critical challenge in both residential and industrial sectors. Our project, "Smart Wireless Water Meter with Web DB," leverages IoT technology to provide an efficient, real-time water monitoring system. This system uses wireless sensors to measure water consumption and transmit the data to a cloud-based web database. Users can access detailed insights, such as real-time water usage, flow rate, and consumption history, via a web interface. The system also enables remote monitoring and alerts for leaks or abnormal usage patterns, helping to prevent water wastage and reduce costs. With its automated tracking, data analytics, and remote access, this smart water meter ensures a sustainable and efficient water management system for modern households and industries.
